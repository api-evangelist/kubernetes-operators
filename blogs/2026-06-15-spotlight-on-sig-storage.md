An interview with Xing Yang, Co-Chair of SIG Storage at VMware by Broadcom, covering the group's history, features shipping in recent Kubernetes releases, and how storage is evolving for AI workloads. Topics include VolumeGroupSnapshot, CSI Changed Block Tracking, the Container Object Storage Interface (COSI), and the challenges of running stateful workloads on Kubernetes.
